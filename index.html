Welcome!!!

Services Offered

Exuberant Camp parents are always pleased with the array of amenities available at camp. All breakfast, lunches and snacks are included in the cost of each camp, and our full-time nurse is an asset to the health and safety of our campers. Additional options for Extended Hours, Transportation, and Supplemental activities that your kid is not signed up for are available for an additional fee. We are happy to provide a variety of options to help you build the best camp service experience available.

Schedule

Exuberant Camp is open Monday-Friday, through the hours of 8am-4pm. Respective pick-ups are 4pm-5pm, parents or family-memebers/friends on the campers emergency pick-up/contact list are allowed to pick their kid up early at anytime. Breakfast is served 8am-9:15am, lunch is served 12pm-1pm, and snack are always available with one snack per hour. Once you register an additional more detailed schedule will be mailed/e-mailed to you the parent about your kids activites.
