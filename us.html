About Us

Company Used

Exuberant Camp is funded through a company we are using called I.I.W.I.I Inc.. They support always helping kids get more education and being able to explore the vast world they live in. If you need help with funding Exuberant Camp suggest going through with I.I.W.I.I Inc. you can call them at 888-555-7777 or email them at ItIsWhatItIs@gmail.com.

Staff

In all programs, the groups are led by college-aged or older staff members who are assisted by Junior and Co-Counselors who are rising sophomores through graduates of high school. All program areas are managed by one or more trained specialists who provide a progression of skills during the camping season. All of the Exuberant Camp senior staff members, including travel camp, specialty camp and athletic camp instructors, are experienced and well trained. A registered nurse is available during the regular camp hours. The nurse oversees any medication required by campers and contacts parents if a child becomes ill during the camp day.

Schedule

Exuberant Camp is open Monday-Friday, through the hours of 8am-4pm. Respective pick-ups are 4pm-5pm, parents or family-memebers/friends on the campers emergency pick-up/contact list are allowed to pick their kid up early at anytime. Breakfast is served 8am-9:15am, lunch is served 12pm-1pm, and snack are always available with one snack per hour. Once you register an additional more detailed schedule will be mailed/e-mailed to you the parent about your kids activites.
